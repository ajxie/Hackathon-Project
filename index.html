First: Name your turtle! (We named ours Shelly!)

shelly = turtle.Turtle()
shelly.shape("turtle")

Then, decide on two colors: one for the drawing, and one for you turtle

star.color('pink', 'blue')

Then, teach your new friend how to move!
Here are some things it can do:

Forward(distance): Moves the turtle forward for a certain distance
(100 is a big distance and 10 is really small!)

shelly.forward(100)

Left(angle): Turn your friend to the left

shelly.left(100)

Right(angle): Turn your friend to the right

shelly.right(100)

Don't lead your friend astray - turn him the right way!

You can make your own turtle or try ours!
